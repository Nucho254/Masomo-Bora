Our mission

We built Masomo Bora because revision shouldn't feel like a photocopy.

Teachers spend hours writing and re-marking revision papers by hand. Students lose their sheets, or wait days for feedback. Masomo Bora keeps every question, every score, and every class in one place — so teachers can teach, and students can actually learn from their mistakes.
